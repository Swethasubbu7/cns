OPHTHALMOLOGY DEPARTMENT

Expert Ophthalmology Care at CNS Super Speciality Hospital in Tiruppur

At CNS Super Speciality Hospital in Tiruppur, our Ophthalmology Department is committed to delivering comprehensive and advanced eye care services. Our experienced ophthalmologists use modern diagnostic tools and advanced treatment techniques to protect, restore, and enhance vision for patients of all ages.

Conditions We Treat

Our Ophthalmology Department in Tiruppur specializes in diagnosing and managing a wide range of eye conditions with precision and personalized care.

Refractive Errors

Correction of nearsightedness (myopia), farsightedness (hyperopia), astigmatism, and presbyopia for improved vision clarity.

Cataracts

Advanced diagnosis and surgical treatment for cataracts to restore clear and comfortable vision.

Glaucoma

Expert management of intraocular pressure to prevent optic nerve damage and preserve eyesight.

Macular Degeneration

Specialized care for age-related macular degeneration (AMD) affecting central vision.

Diabetic Retinopathy

Comprehensive treatment for retinal damage caused by diabetes to prevent vision loss.

Advanced Diagnostic and Treatment Options

Our Ophthalmology Department in Tiruppur is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective eye care treatments.

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)

High-resolution imaging of the retina and optic nerve for early detection and monitoring of eye diseases.

Corneal Topography

Detailed mapping of the cornea to support precise diagnosis and customized treatment planning.

Microsurgical Techniques

Minimally invasive surgical procedures for cataracts, glaucoma, and other eye conditions.

Artificial Tears and Medications

Prescription treatments to manage chronic eye conditions and improve eye comfort.
