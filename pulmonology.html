PULMONOLOGY

Comprehensive Pulmonology Care at at CNS Super Speciality Hospital

At CNS Super Speciality Hospital, our Pulmonology Department is dedicated to diagnosing and treating a wide range of respiratory and lung-related conditions. Our experienced pulmonologists combine advanced technology with evidence-based treatments to deliver accurate diagnosis, effective care, and improved respiratory health for every patient.

Conditions We Treat

Our pulmonology specialists provide expert care for both acute and chronic respiratory disorders, ensuring comprehensive and personalized treatment.

Asthma

Long-term asthma management focused on symptom control, prevention of attacks, and improved quality of life.

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

Comprehensive care for COPD, including emphysema and chronic bronchitis, to improve breathing and daily function.

Pulmonary Fibrosis

Specialized treatment for lung scarring conditions to slow disease progression and enhance respiratory capacity.

Sleep Apnea

Diagnosis and treatment of obstructive sleep apnea and other sleep- related breathing disorders.

Lung Infections & Pneumonia

Effective management of bacterial, viral, and fungal lung infections including pneumonia.

Tuberculosis (TB)

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of tuberculosis, including drug-resistant TB cases.

Lung Cancer

Multidisciplinary approach to early diagnosis, staging, and treatment planning for lung cancer.

Pulmonary Hypertension

Advanced care for high blood pressure in the lungs to support heart and lung function.

Advanced Diagnostic and Treatment Options

Our Pulmonology Department is equipped with modern diagnostic tools and advanced treatment methods to ensure accurate evaluation and effective respiratory care.

Pulmonary Function Tests (PFTs)

Comprehensive lung function testing to diagnose and monitor respiratory conditions.

Bronchoscopy

Minimally invasive procedure to examine airways and diagnose lung diseases with precision.

Advanced Imaging

Detailed evaluation using chest X-rays, CT scans, and other imaging techniques for accurate diagnosis.

Medication Management

Personalized treatment plans using advanced medications tailored to individual respiratory conditions.

Oxygen & Ventilatory Support

Advanced oxygen therapy and respiratory support for patients with chronic and critical lung conditions.

Personalized Care and Support

Our pulmonology team follows a patient-centered approach, designing individualized treatment plans based on each patient’s condition, lifestyle, and long-term health goals.

We also educate patients and families about disease management, preventive care, and lifestyle modifications, while offering compassionate support throughout every stage of treatment and recovery.
