PSYCHIATRY

Comprehensive Psychiatry Care at CNS Super Speciality Hospital

At CNS Super Speciality Hospital, our Psychiatry Department is dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based care for a wide range of mental health conditions. Our experienced team of psychiatrists focuses on accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment, and long-term mental well-being using modern therapeutic approaches.

Conditions We Treat

Our psychiatry specialists manage mental health conditions across all age groups with sensitivity, confidentiality, and clinical excellence.

Depression

Diagnosis and treatment of major depressive disorder and other mood disorders affecting emotional and daily functioning.

Anxiety Disorders

Comprehensive care for anxiety, panic disorder, social anxiety, and generalized anxiety disorder.

Bipolar Disorder

Structured treatment plans for bipolar I and II disorders to manage mood fluctuations and prevent relapse.

Schizophrenia

Long-term management and support for schizophrenia and related psychotic disorders.

Sleep Disorders

Evaluation and treatment of insomnia and other sleep-related mental health conditions.

Child, Adolescent & Geriatric Psychiatry

Age-specific mental health care addressing emotional, behavioral, and cognitive concerns across life stages.

ADHD

Diagnosis and treatment of attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder in children and adults.

Advanced Treatment and Therapeutic Options

Our Psychiatry Department uses proven and advanced treatment methods to deliver effective mental health care tailored to individual needs.

Medication Management

Personalized medication plans with regular monitoring to improve symptoms and overall quality of life.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based psychotherapy focused on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors.

Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT)

Safe and effective treatment for severe depression and selected psychiatric conditions when indicated.

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

Non-invasive brain stimulation therapy for treatment-resistant depression and related disorders.

Personalized Care and Support

We follow a patient-centered approach that respects each individual’s emotional, psychological, and social needs. Treatment plans are customized and reviewed regularly to ensure effective outcomes.

Our psychiatry team also provides guidance and education to families, helping them understand conditions and treatment pathways while offering compassionate support throughout the recovery journey.
