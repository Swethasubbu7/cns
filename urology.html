Urology

Comprehensive Urological Care at CNS Super Speciality Hospital

At CNS Super Speciality Hospital, our Urology Department is dedicated to providing advanced, compassionate care for all Urology conditions. Our team of expert urologists uses the latest technology and treatment methods to deliver personalized care tailored to each patient's unique needs.

Conditions We Treat

Our urology specialists diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions affecting the urinary tract and male reproductive system, including:

  • Kidney Stones

    Diagnosis and treatment of kidney stones using minimally invasive techniques such as shock wave lithotripsy (SWL) and ureteroscopy.

  • Prostate Health

    Management of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), prostate cancer screening, diagnosis, and treatment.

  • Bladder Conditions

    Treatment for bladder infections, interstitial cystitis, bladder cancer, and overactive bladder.

  • Male Sexual Health

    Addressing erectile dysfunction, Peyronie's disease, and low testosterone levels.

  • Urinary Incontinence

    Offering solutions for stress incontinence, urge incontinence, and overflow incontinence in both men and women.

  • Pediatric Urology

    Specialized care for children with congenital and acquired urological conditions, including vesicoureteral reflux and hypospadias.

  • Male Infertility

    Diagnosis and treatment of male infertility, including varicocele repair and sperm retrieval techniques.

Advanced Diagnostic and Treatment Options

Our Urology Department is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment. Services include:

  • Endoscopic Procedures

    Minimally invasive techniques such as cystoscopy and ureteroscopy for diagnosis and treatment.

  • Imaging Services

    Advanced imaging modalities like ultrasound, CT scans, and MRI for detailed evaluation.

  • Robotic Surgery

    Precision robotic-assisted surgery for complex urological procedures, offering reduced recovery times and improved outcomes.

  • Laser Surgery

    Use of laser technology for the treatment of kidney stones, BPH, and other urological conditions.

  • Biopsy Services

    Prostate and bladder biopsies for the diagnosis of cancers and other conditions.

Personalized Care and Support

At CNS Super Speciality Hospital, we understand that urological issues can be sensitive and affect quality of life. Our team is committed to providing compassionate care and support throughout your treatment journey. We work closely with each patient to develop a customized treatment plan that meets their specific needs and goals.
